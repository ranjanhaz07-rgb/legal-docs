# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 14, 2026

Nexyn Bot values user privacy and only collects information necessary for the proper functioning of its services.

## Information We May Collect

Depending on the features used, Nexyn Bot may store:

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server IDs
* Channel IDs
* Role IDs
* Command usage information
* Server-specific configuration settings
* Conversation history required for chatbot functionality

## How Information Is Used

Collected information is used solely to:

* Provide and maintain bot features.
* Save server preferences and settings.
* Improve performance and prevent abuse.
* Enable chatbot-related functionality.

## Data Sharing

Nexyn Bot does not sell personal information. Data will only be disclosed when required by applicable law or to protect the integrity and security of the service.

## Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored data; however, no system can guarantee absolute security.

## Third-Party Services

Some features may rely on third-party services. Nexyn Bot is not responsible for the policies or practices of those external services.

## Data Removal

Server administrators may request the removal of server-related data where applicable.

## Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be modified at any time. Continued use of Nexyn Bot after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
